Why we exist

Translating research into real impact

We operate under academic leadership at the University of Roehampton, convening researchers, SMEs, NGOs and community organisations to deliver applied sustainability initiatives across London. Each project is evidence-led and community-rooted.

Evidence-led action
Translating sustainability research into frameworks communities can actually use.
Cross-sector collaboration
Bridging academia, SMEs, NGOs and local authorities for joined-up impact.
Community empowerment
Building local capacity to lead the transition to a fair, green economy.
Measurable outcomes
Robust frameworks to track social value, skills and environmental impact.

✦ Completed pilot · Merton
Empowering Change: Volunteer Champion Local Sustainability Action in Merton
A pioneering initiative where University of Roehampton students contributed to urban gardening, zero-waste food distribution and intergenerational community engagement across Merton — in partnership with Sustainable Merton. Led by Prof. Ayşe Demir, funded via BIG South London Innovation Vouchers (UKSPF).
Tabled in UK Parliament as Early Day Motion no. 1753 by Paul Kohler MP — recognising national significance in boosting intergenerational volunteering and youth civic engagement. 18 parliamentary signatures.
